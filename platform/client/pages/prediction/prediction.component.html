How it works

Build your XI, get the prediction

ScoutAI uses a Logistic Regression model trained on individual player performance data from the Portuguese Primeira Liga to calculate the probability of each match outcome.

🏟️

1. Browse a Squad

Select any Primeira Liga team to load their squad. You can switch teams as many times as you want to build a mixed starting XI.

👕

2. Pick 11 Starters

Search and add 11 players per side. Players are automatically sorted by position: GK → DEF → MID → FWD.

📊

3. Get the Prediction

Hit "Predict" and the model returns the probability of a home win, draw, or away win based on historical player data.
